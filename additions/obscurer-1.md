# Enchanted Book Bundle

The Enchanted Book Bundle can be right clicked to unveil a random boosted enchanted book\
(such as sharpness 6 or protection 5)

### Obtaining

It can be crafted with 4 Shattered Orbs of Origin and 1 Random Enchanted Book

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (2).png>)

### Usage

Here is the list of boosted enchantments the book can give:

* Sharpness
* Smite
* Bane of Arthropods
* Protection
* Projectile Protection
* Fire Protection
* Blast Protection
* Efficiency
* Fortune
* Riptide
* Fire Aspect
* Thorns
* Feather Falling
* Looting
* Power
* Knockback
