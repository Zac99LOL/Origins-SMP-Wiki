# Delusion

Gameplay Impact: \[<mark style="color:$success;">⬤</mark>⬤⬤] Origin Difficulty \[<mark style="color:$success;">⬤</mark>⬤⬤]

<mark style="color:$primary;">Utility</mark>/<mark style="color:green;">Tank</mark>\
This origin is better at literally everything. It takes less damage, has more health, among so many other buffs! With only one small downside! (whats not to love!)

<summary>(Slightly) Stronger</summary>

+20% To all positive stats.

* All that stats that are amplified are:

  * Armor Bars
  * Armor Toughness
  * Knockback
  * Attack Speed
  * Knockback Resistance
  * Movement Speed
  * Reach
  * Mining Speed
  * Damage Dealt
  * Damage Resistance
  * Hunger Loss (-20%)
  * Swim Speed
  * Experience Gain
  * Immunity Frames
  * Mob Breeding Cooldown
  * Step Height
  * Mob Visibility (-20%)
  * Mob Drops
  * Elytra Speed
  * Natural Regeneration
  * Repair Cost (-20%)
  * Totem Fail Chance (-20%)

</details>

</details>
