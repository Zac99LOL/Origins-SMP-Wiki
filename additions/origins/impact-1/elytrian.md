# Elytrian

Gameplay Impact: \[<mark style="color:yellow;">⬤⬤</mark>⬤] Origin Difficulty \[<mark style="color:$success;">⬤</mark>⬤⬤]

<mark style="color:purple;">Movement</mark>/<mark style="color:blue;">Single-Target</mark>\
This origin is designed for fast-paced movement and close range pvp. Being able to fly, dash, and hover while being immune to fall damage, and being able to deal high melee damage when flying. All while taking more melee and fire damage, promoting a much more risky playstyle.

* Recommended Tip: When flying with an elytra, try to keep out of melee range and land consistent bow shots while flying.

<details>

<summary>Winged</summary>

You have an permanent elytra attached to you. (Doesn't affect wearing chestplates)

* Toggle with \[Secondary Key], (2s Cooldown)
* If elytra is toggled off, then you can instead hold space to slowfall.
* When initiating elytra flight, you boost forward like you used your dash ability.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Air Dash</summary>

Blast yourself forward with air. Using 1 Dash Charge. (2 Charges Max, 5s Per Charge Refill)

</details>

<details>

<summary>Updraft</summary>

When jumping while sprinting, you use the air around you to jump much higher then you originally would've.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Light as a feather</summary>

You run like the wind, granting +20% Movement Speed.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Like Air</summary>

You use the air around you to negate all incoming fall damage.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Surfing Bird</summary>

When wearing a leather cap, players can right click you to ride on your head.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Artistic Elytras</summary>

By applying certain items to an elytra in your inventory with right click, you can customize the elytras appearance and trail.

* The custom elytras (and items required to get the textures) are as follows:\
  <mark style="color:yellow;">Gilded - Yellow Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:$info;">Feathered - Light Gray Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:orange;">Creaking - Orange Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:purple;">Sculk - Blue Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:blue;">Flow - Cyan Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:$danger;">Cherry - Pink Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:$primary;">Prism - Light Blue Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:purple;">Amethyst - Purple Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:green;">Lush - Green Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:$info;">Raven - Gray Dye</mark>
* To change your own elytra, you instead must eat the dye, you can remove the texture by eating a white dye.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Fragile Body</summary>

Your excessive manipulation of air has left you weak, resulting in 50% more damage taken from all melee attacks.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Kinetic Multiplication</summary>

You take 75% more kinetic damage.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Excessive Flames</summary>

The wind around you amplifies incoming fire damage by 2x.

* While on fire you also cannot fly, and cannot dash, do not jump higher, cannot slow fall, and run at normal speed.
* This effect is nullfied when you have fire resistance.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Firework Exhaustion</summary>

You cannot use fireworks to boost yourself while flying.

</details>
