# Spelunker

Gameplay Impact: \[<mark style="color:$success;">⬤</mark>⬤⬤] Origin Difficulty \[<mark style="color:$success;">⬤</mark>⬤⬤]

<mark style="color:$primary;">Utility</mark>/<mark style="color:purple;">Gatherer</mark>\
This origin, although simple, has the ability to obtain mass amounts of resources easily, being able to locate and collect ores much faster then any other origin can, with its only downsides being quite minor.

<details>

<summary>Ore Sensing</summary>

When using your primary without sneaking, you can switch between 3 ore meters, these each detect nearvy powdered ores, metallic ores, and crystalline ores respectively, with the meter filling up the closer you are to an ore (within 7 blocks) from that category.

* **Metallic Ores**\
  (Metallic Meter) checks for:\
  \- Iron\
  \- Copper\
  \- Gold\
  \- Ancient debris
* <mark style="color:$info;">**Powdered Ores**</mark>\
  (Gunpowder Meter) checks for:\
  \- Coal\
  \- Redstone\
  \- Lapis\
  \- Zinc
* <mark style="color:$success;">**Crystalline Ores**</mark>\
  (Emerald Meter) checks for:\
  \- Emerald\
  \- Diamond\
  \- Quartz\
  \- Amethyst

</details>

<details>

<summary>Spelunker's Vision</summary>

After all your time spent in the mines, you can see everything as if it was fully illuminated. (Can be toggled with primary key while sneaking)

</details>

<details>

<summary>Spelunker's Backpack</summary>

A backpack for storing items. When changing origin, any items in the backpack go into your inventory. (9 Total Slots, They do not drop on Death)

* Open with \[Secondary Key]
* Cannot be opened within 10s of taking damage.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Spelunker's Speed</summary>

All blocks that are best mined with a pickaxe can be mined 50% faster.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Spelunker's Fortune</summary>

When mining with a pickaxe, gain 1 level of fortune.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Pickaxe Power</summary>

All pickaxes deal +20% damage per effiency level.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Mining Makeover</summary>

By applying certain items to a pickaxe in your inventory with right click, you can customize the pickaxes appearance and sounds.

* The custom pickaxes (and items required to get the textures) are as follows:\
  <mark style="color:blue;">Toothpick - Cyan Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:yellow;">Electro-Charged Pick - Yellow Dye</mark>\
  <mark style="color:purple;">Gemstone Drill - Purple Dye</mark>

</details>

<details>

<summary>Tightened Speed</summary>

You move 2x as fast can jump up full blocks when crawling.

</details>

<details>

<summary>One Trick Pony</summary>

You mine blocks that aren't mined with a pickaxe 30% slower.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Spelunker's Appetite</summary>

Due to your heavy duty work, you lose hunger 60% faster.

</details>
