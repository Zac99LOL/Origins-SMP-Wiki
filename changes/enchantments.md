# Enchantments

Breach and Punch have been removed, and have been respectively replaced with Stagger and Leech.

**Stagger: (1 - 4)**\
Inflicts one level of slowness (per level) for 4 seconds when hitting entities with a mace slam.

**Leech: (1 - 2)**\
Grants bows self healing, but puts them on a brief cooldown after shooting.
