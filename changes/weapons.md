# Weapons

The server has 2 new weapons added, these are the Anchorblade and the Scythe. The Anchorblade can be thrown and comes back to you when landing. While the scythe will pull enemies in and stun them on a crit. In addition to this, the Mace and Wind Charge are also backported from 1.21.
